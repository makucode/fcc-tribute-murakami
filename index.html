Biography

Haruki Murakami is an award winning Japanese author. His publications have been bestsellers in Japan as well as internationally, with his work translated into more than 50 languages and selling millions of copies worldwide. He was born in 1949 in Kyoto and grew up in Kobe before moving to Tokyo to attend Waseda University, he published his first novel "Hear the Wind Sing" in 1979 at the age of 30, after working as the owner of a small jazz bar for seven years. His most known works include the novels "Norwegian Wood", "The Wind-Up Bird Chronicle", "Kafka on the Shore" and "1Q84".
